About me

I am an environmental health researcher who loves programming in R. My current work is on air pollution exposure assessment using both stationary and mobile platforms. Apart from being a researcher, I am also a co-founder, manager, educator and a mentor. I collaborate with scientists from many global and local organizations including my alma-mater, University of Washington.

My early career was as a Biomedical Researcher where I learnt how to design experiments, write papers and analyze data. I later transitioned to Environmental Health and worked on childhood lead poisoning and air quality in India, US, Africa and south Asia. Check out my global CV here. I also discovered R in grad school and have never looked back. Through this website I hope to share my learning journey with you.
